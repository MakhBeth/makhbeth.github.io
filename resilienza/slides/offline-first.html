Offline First

The site should work even offline or with an unstable network.

  • Service Workers — intercept requests, serve from cache
  • Cache API — store responses for offline use
  • PWA — installable, resilient, native-like

Even simple strategies make a huge difference:

  • <link rel="preload"> — critical resources first
  • <link rel="prefetch"> — next page, ready before the click
  • Stale-while-revalidate — show cached, update in background
